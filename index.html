What is Droom?

Droom is a powerful new job matching platform which has combined the best features of legacy platforms in order to make it as easy as possible to find your dream job. Signup now and start matching with employers in mere minutes.

How It Works

To get started, signup and fill in the relevant information to help attract your dream employer. If you already have a LinkedIn account you can import your data or upload a resume to give your profile some extra pop!

Droom helps match job seekers with exciting employers by utilizing a "swiping" feature similar to popular dating apps. Both job seekers and employers will swipe until a match is found at which point the employer can decide to continue with the hiring process.

Droom fine tunes jobs suggested to users and profiles sugested to employers through the use of our proprietary AI matching algorythm. We have spent countless hours perfecting this system to ensure using Droom feels like a dream.
